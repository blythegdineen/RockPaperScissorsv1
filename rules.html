Rules

The game of P, R, S is based on trust, skill and determination. Each player must:
  • Prepare, with their hand in a fist, to make their move
  • Throw one of three options: rock (fist), paper (flat hand), scissors (two extended fingers)
  • The winner is determined based on these guidelines: rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, paper beats rock, and the same item is a tie
Official rules are on the World RPS Society website.
Blythe Dineen, December 2018.
